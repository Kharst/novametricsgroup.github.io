The Platform

A complete intelligence
stack for solar

Solar Intelligence is a decision-engineering platform built on deep domain knowledge. Every module is engineered to produce outputs that are defensible, accurate, and immediately deployable in a client context.

We understand that solar data is structured, repeatable, and forecastable — and we've built a system that exploits that precision at scale.

☀
Yield Forecasting Engine
Location-based irradiance data modelled by panel type, degradation curves, and system losses into monthly and annual production forecasts.
⚡
Load-Based Sizing

🔋
Storage Intelligence
Battery capacity modelling, charge/discharge simulation, backup duration analysis, and time-of-use tariff optimisation.
📊
Financial Modelling

Proposal Automation

Portfolio Intelligence

Decision Engineering

Built on data.
Designed for precision.

Each module of Solar Intelligence transforms raw inputs into structured, actionable outputs — no manual spreadsheets, no guesswork.

01
Irradiance to revenue
We model solar generation from location-specific irradiance data and trace it through every system variable — panel type, orientation, inverter efficiency, temperature coefficients — to an annual kWh output you can defend.
Yield Forecasting
02
Consumption-aligned sizing
System size is derived from actual load profiles and consumption data, not rules of thumb. Oversized or undersized proposals erode trust — our engine eliminates that risk with structured sizing logic.
System Sizing
03
Investment-grade financials
Tariff escalation, inflation assumptions, IRR, NPV and sensitivity analysis baked into every project. We model the difference between "static payback" and real-world compounding value over 25 years.
Financial Modelling
04
Battery precision
Backup duration, load coverage, depth-of-discharge modelling, and round-trip efficiency — storage is modelled against real autonomy requirements, not estimated from capacity alone.
Storage Intelligence
05
Automated proposal engine
Every number that comes out of the modelling engine feeds directly into a branded, structured proposal. AI-generated commentary explains the system technically and commercially. One click, client-ready.
Proposal Automation
06
Engagement analytics
Proposals track open rates, view events, download events, and client engagement signals — giving installers a pipeline intelligence layer that goes beyond just sending a PDF.
