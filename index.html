Flagship Platform

Solar
Intelligence

Not a calculator. Not a report template. Solar Intelligence is a predictive energy modelling engine — purpose-built for the people who size, sell, finance, and operate solar assets in the real world.

It ingests your system specifications, location, load profile, and financial parameters — and returns a complete intelligence picture: what the asset will produce, what it will store, what it will earn, and how it compares against every alternative configuration.

  • Predictive yield intelligence at hourly resolution — not estimates, not averages
  • Battery dispatch and storage utilisation modelling against real load curves
  • Full financial sensitivity: payback, IRR, NPV, tariff and escalation scenarios
  • Grid export optimisation and time-of-use arbitrage modelling
  • Portfolio-level intelligence for multi-site operators and utility planners
  • Automated, client-ready intelligence reports — on demand or scheduled
