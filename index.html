Decision Engineering

Designed to produce the
right answer every time

Every number in a Nova Metrics proposal is derived from structured engineering models — not rules of thumb or spreadsheet estimates. Defensible, repeatable, and aligned with South African standards by design.

01
Load-based solar sizing
Solar is sized to cover daytime consumption plus overnight battery recharge — not a rough multiple of peak load. Required energy is calculated using industry-standard performance ratios and system availability benchmarks appropriate for the South African context, then matched to the most suitable panel configuration from your live catalogue.
Solar Array
02
LiFePO4 battery design validation
The platform applies strict multi-gate engineering checks before accepting a battery configuration: capacity headroom relative to the evening load requirement, depth-of-discharge within design targets for LiFePO4 longevity, and guaranteed autonomy runtime. Designs are optimised to maximise battery cycle life. Industry-standard round-trip efficiency is applied throughout.
Battery
03
NRS 097-2-1 alignment check
The inverter is sized with an engineering safety margin applied to peak load. The DC/AC ratio is validated in alignment with NRS 097-2-1 parameters — ensuring the design supports compliance with South African grid interconnection requirements. Every uploaded inverter is also automatically cross-referenced against the South African Master Inverter Compliance Register (2,440+ certified models).
Inverter · NRS 097-2-1
04
365-day net yield modelling
Annual production estimates apply performance ratios, system availability factors, and panel degradation assumptions aligned with South African industry norms. Location-specific irradiance data is drawn from a database covering 150+ South African locations. Specific yield is benchmarked against national performance references as a quality indicator.
Yield
05
Engineering-grade financial projections
25-year indicative projections are built using NERSA-aligned tariff escalation scenarios, industry-standard discount rates for NPV analysis, IRR modelling, and full lifecycle costing — including maintenance provisions, insurance estimates, and battery replacement assumptions. All financial outputs are estimates and will vary based on actual site conditions, tariff changes, usage patterns, and financing structure.
NPV · IRR · Financials
06
Engagement pipeline intelligence
After a proposal is sent, the platform tracks email opens, proposal views, PDF downloads, and estimated time-on-page — subject to user consent and applicable privacy policies. Installers see a follow-up priority signal — Hot, Warm, Cold — so they know exactly who to contact and when.
