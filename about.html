About Nova Metrics

Solar intelligence
is our only business.

We don't do general analytics. We don't build dashboards for every industry. We built a company around a single, specific ambition: to give the solar energy sector the data intelligence it has always needed but never had.

Our Focus

Deep expertise.
One domain.

Nova Metrics was founded on a conviction: that the solar energy industry makes too many critical decisions on too little data. Proposals are built on generic assumptions. Investments are modelled on spreadsheet rules of thumb. System performance is assessed by eye, not by algorithm.

We built Solar Intelligence to change that. Not to visualise data more attractively — but to extract intelligence from it that didn't previously exist. To answer questions that couldn't be answered before without significant time and cost.

Our team brings together deep expertise in data science, energy systems modelling, and predictive analytics — applied exclusively to solar energy. That focus is our edge. It means our models are more specific, our outputs are more accurate, and our understanding of your context is deeper than any generalist can offer.
