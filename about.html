About Nova Metrics

Solar intelligence
is our only business.

Nova Metrics is a specialist solar energy intelligence company. We design and deploy advanced modelling systems that bring precision to system design, yield forecasting, storage optimisation, financial structuring, and portfolio performance across the solar value chain.

Our Focus

Deep expertise.
One domain.

Nova Metrics was founded on a simple conviction: solar decisions deserve engineering-grade intelligence. System sizing, yield projections, storage strategies, and financial returns shape significant capital commitments — and those decisions require structured, defensible modelling.

Solar Intelligence transforms irradiance data, load profiles, tariff structures, degradation curves, and financing variables into decision-grade models. These models quantify risk, simulate multi-scenario outcomes, and generate outputs designed to withstand both technical and financial scrutiny.

Our team combines data science, energy systems modelling, and financial analytics — applied exclusively to solar energy. This depth enables contextual accuracy, stronger modelling fidelity, and intelligence aligned to real-world operating conditions.
