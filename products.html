Solar Intelligence Dashboard

The Solar Intelligence Dashboard helps individuals, installers, and corporates optimize panel sizing, energy usage, storage, and grid contributions. It uses live weather and performance data to calculate power output, energy savings, and return on investment.

  • Live hourly and daily solar performance tracking
  • Cost savings and energy contribution analysis
  • Custom panel sizing and battery optimization
  • Regional solar potential benchmarking
