3. How we use your information

We use personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected. Specifically:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Solar Intelligence platform
  • To generate solar system designs, financial projections, and branded PDF proposals
  • To manage the installation pipeline, job records, and customer handover documentation
  • To send proposals by email on behalf of installers and track delivery and engagement
  • To send account-related communications (billing, security, product updates) to installers
  • To prevent fraud, ensure platform security, and comply with legal obligations
  • To improve the platform based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns

We do not sell personal information to any third party. We do not use personal information for direct marketing to end-clients without their explicit consent.

5. Proposal engagement tracking

When an installer sends a proposal to their client through the platform, we track the following engagement events: whether the proposal email was delivered, when it was opened, how many times the PDF was viewed, and when it was downloaded.

This tracking exists to help the installer know when to follow up with their client. It uses standard email tracking technology (a small transparent image embedded in the email).

If you have received a Nova Metrics proposal from a solar installer: you have been informed of this tracking by the disclosure in the proposal email footer. If you would like this tracking disabled for your proposal, contact the installer who sent it or email us at info@novametricsgroup.com and we will disable it for your record.

7. How we protect your information

We take the security of personal information seriously. Our technical and organisational measures include:

  • Row Level Security (RLS) on all database tables — each installer can only access their own data. No installer can view any other installer's client records, proposals, or job data.
  • Authentication via Supabase Auth with industry-standard password hashing. We do not store plain-text passwords.
  • Encrypted transmission — all data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access controls — platform access is limited to authenticated users only. Administrative access to the database is restricted to authorised Nova Metrics personnel.
  • Regular security reviews of database policies and access controls.

Despite these measures, no internet-based system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in harm to data subjects, we will notify affected parties and the Information Regulator as required by POPIA within the required timeframes.

8. Your rights as a data subject

Under POPIA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access: You may request a description of the personal information we hold about you and how it is being used.
  • Right to correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate, irrelevant, excessive, out-of-date, or misleading information.
  • Right to deletion: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Right to object: You may object to our processing of your personal information on grounds of legitimate interest, including the proposal engagement tracking described in Section 5.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights under POPIA have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Information Officer at info@novametricsgroup.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Responsibilities of installers using the platform

Installers who use Solar Intelligence to process their clients' personal information bear the following responsibilities:

  • You must have a lawful basis for entering your clients' personal information into the platform — typically their consent or a legitimate interest in providing them with a solar proposal.
  • If asked by a client how their information is being used, you must be able to explain that it is processed through the Nova Metrics Solar Intelligence platform and handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
  • You are responsible for the accuracy of the information you enter about your clients.
  • You must not enter personal information into the platform for any purpose other than managing solar proposals and installations.
