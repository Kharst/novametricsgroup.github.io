1. Scope of Service

Nova Metrics Solar Intelligence ("the Platform") is a software-as-a-service tool designed to assist registered solar installers and energy consultants in South Africa to size, design, quote, and propose photovoltaic and battery backup systems for residential and commercial clients.

The Platform is accessed via novametricsgroup.com and associated subpages. Access is provided to registered installer accounts only. End clients interact with client-facing pages (proposal views, design approval pages) generated on their behalf by a registered installer.

B2B SaaS Installer-facing South Africa

2. Nature of the Tool — Design Assistance, Not Certification

The Platform produces recommended system designs based on engineering algorithms aligned with South African standards. It does not produce certified electrical designs or constitute a professional engineering sign-off.

What the engine does

  • Sizes inverters against NRS 097 continuous load requirements and startup surge profiles
  • Sizes battery capacity against autonomy requirements using DoD-adjusted usable kWh
  • Sizes PV arrays against regional irradiance, system PR and degradation curves
  • Models 25-year financial returns using published NERSA tariff escalation data

What the engine does not do

  • Replace a physical site assessment by a qualified person
  • Constitute an electrical compliance certificate (CoC) or engineering certificate
  • Account for site-specific shading, orientation deviations, or non-standard wiring without installer input

The licensed installer is the responsible party for verifying all designs against actual site conditions before installation. The post-site-assessment revision workflow within the Platform is designed to facilitate this.

Design tool only Not a CoC substitute

3. What Data We Collect & Why

Installer account data

  • Name, email address, company name, contact number — required for account authentication and proposal branding
  • Company logo and brand colours — used to generate branded client proposals

Client data (collected by installers on behalf of their clients)

  • Client name, email address, phone number, physical address — required to generate and deliver proposals

  • Approval decisions and timestamps — recorded for audit trail purposes

System usage data

  • Proposal views (open tracking pixel) — to inform installers when clients view proposals
  • Approval page interactions — timestamps of when clients open, respond to, or leave approval pages

We do not collect payment card data. We do not sell data to third parties. We do not use client data for marketing purposes.

4. Where Data Is Stored

All platform data is stored on Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL database service hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the eu-west-1 (Ireland) region.

Supabase holds SOC 2 Type II certification. Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).

Proposal PDFs are stored in Supabase Storage (S3-compatible). Email delivery is handled by Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), a GDPR-compliant email infrastructure provider headquartered in the EU.

5. Security Measures

Authentication

  • Installer accounts are protected by Supabase Auth (email + password with bcrypt hashing)
  • Session tokens are short-lived JWTs. Password reset requires email verification

API access

  • The browser-exposed API key (anon key) has row-level-security enforced — it cannot access other installers' data
  • All sensitive operations (proposal generation, email dispatch, PDF storage) run through server-side edge functions using the service role key, which never reaches the browser

Client approval links

  • Each approval link contains a cryptographically random UUID token (128-bit entropy)
  • Tokens are single-use and tied to a specific site visit record
  • Once a client responds, the responded_at timestamp is written, preventing status changes without a confirmed button click

What we do not do

  • We do not log proposal content to any third-party analytics service
  • We do not use cookies for tracking. The open-tracking pixel is a 1×1 image request to our own edge function only

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Proposal and client records are retained for the duration of an active installer account plus 24 months thereafter, to support warranty, SETA audit, and dispute resolution requirements common in the solar installation industry.

If an installer account is closed, the account holder may request full data deletion by contacting us. Deletion is completed within 30 days. PDF files in storage are purged as part of the same process.

Clients whose data was captured by an installer may request access to or deletion of their personal information by contacting the installer directly, or Nova Metrics if the installer account is no longer active.
