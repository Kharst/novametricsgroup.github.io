4. Critical disclaimer — financial outputs are indicative estimates only

This section is important. Please read it carefully.

All financial outputs generated by the Platform — including but not limited to estimated annual savings, payback periods, net present value (NPV), internal rate of return (IRR), and 25-year financial projections — are indicative estimates only.

These outputs are based on:

  • Information entered by the Installer (load profile, tariff rate, tariff escalation assumption, system cost)
  • Industry-standard assumptions about system performance, degradation, and availability
  • Modelled tariff escalation scenarios based on NERSA guidelines and historical data
  • Simplified financial models that do not account for all variables affecting actual returns

Actual results will differ from projected outputs due to real-world variables including but not limited to: actual electricity consumption patterns, changes in utility tariffs, system downtime, local weather variation, financing costs, insurance claims, and battery replacement timing.

Nova Metrics outputs are not financial advice as defined under the Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act, 2002 (FAIS). Installers must not present platform outputs to end-clients as guaranteed returns, investment advice, or certified financial projections. You are responsible for communicating the indicative and estimated nature of all financial outputs to your clients before they rely on them to make purchasing decisions.

Nova Metrics accepts no liability for any loss suffered by an Installer or an End-Client arising from reliance on financial outputs generated by the Platform.
