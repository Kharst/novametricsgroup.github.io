Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Solar Intelligence — the platform, your trial, billing, and how we handle your data.

☀ Getting Started
What is Solar Intelligence? ▾

Solar Intelligence is an AI-powered solar proposal and system sizing platform built for South African solar installers. It lets you design NRS 097-2-1 compliant PV systems — selecting the right panels, inverters, and batteries for a client's load — and generate a branded PDF proposal in seconds.

It's built and operated by Nova Metrics Group (Pty) Ltd, based in Rivonia, Johannesburg.

How do I get started? ▾

Click Register Free. Enter your company name and email — no credit card required. You'll receive a login link by email. Click it, set your password, and you're in. The whole process takes under two minutes.

What's included in the free trial? ▾

Your 60-day free trial includes full access to all Solar Intelligence features:

  • Unlimited system designs (solar array, battery, inverter sizing)
  • NRS 097-2-1 compliance checks on every design
  • Up to 5 AI-generated client proposals (residential and commercial)
  • Access to the shared hardware database (panels, inverters, batteries)
  • Load Builder with appliance catalog and quick templates
  • 25-year financial model with NERSA tariff escalation rates

No credit card is required to start. After 60 days, a subscription is required to continue generating proposals.

Can I use my own hardware pricing? ▾

Yes. Once logged in, go to My Hardware to upload your own panel, inverter, and battery pricelist. The system will use your prices for all calculations and proposals. Until you upload your own list, the shared Nova Metrics hardware database is used as a fallback.

💳 Billing & Subscriptions
What happens after my 60-day trial? ▾

After 60 days, your system design tools remain accessible, but generating new AI proposals requires an active subscription. Your data and all previously generated proposals are never deleted. You can subscribe at any time from the Billing page inside the platform.

Can I cancel at any time? ▾

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data encrypted? ▾

Yes. Supabase applies industry-standard encryption to all data stored and transmitted on Nova Metrics' behalf:

  • At rest: AES-256 encryption on all disks and backups. Encryption keys are generated per-project and protected by FIPS 140-2 compliant Hardware Security Modules (HSMs).
  • In transit: TLS 1.2 with modern cipher suites on all connections, including credential transmission.
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups, stored on infrastructure independent of live project resources, with 99.99% availability target.

Supabase's global sub-processors (listed in Schedule 3 of the DPA) may process usage and support metadata in other regions, but your core account and proposal data remains in the af-south-1 region.

⚡ Technical & System Design
What compliance standard do the system designs follow? ▾

Every system design is checked against NRS 097-2-1, the South African grid connection standard for small-scale embedded generation (SSEG). The platform automatically enforces:

  • DC/AC ratio between 1.0 and 1.5 (target 1.3:1)
  • LiFePO4 battery depth of discharge ≤ 85%
  • Inverter sizing at 1.3× peak load (safety margin)
  • Performance Ratio of 80% and System Availability of 97% (SA spec)
  • NERSA SSEG registration thresholds flagged per system size
How accurate are the financial projections? ▾

The 25-year financial model uses NERSA-published tariff escalation rates (8.76% for 2026, 8.83% for 2027, then 5% per annum conservatively). It also accounts for panel degradation (0.55%/year), annual O&M and insurance (2% of capex), and battery replacement at year 10 (70% of battery cost).

NPV is calculated at a 12% discount rate. These are projections — actual savings will depend on your client's real consumption, tariff changes, and system performance. We recommend presenting them as indicative estimates.

Can I use Solar Intelligence on mobile? ▾

The platform is primarily designed for desktop use — you'll get the best experience on a laptop or desktop browser. The registration and login pages are fully mobile-responsive. On-site mobile access to the sizing engine works but is best used in landscape orientation on a tablet or large phone screen.
