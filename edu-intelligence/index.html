Staff & Operations

Staff quality drives retention. But the most critical question for any buyer: is this a real business, or a job disguised as a business?

The dependency trap: Many schools are not businesses — they are the founder. Owner dependency is the single biggest destroyer of acquisition value. A school that cannot run without its founder is essentially unsellable at a fair price.
